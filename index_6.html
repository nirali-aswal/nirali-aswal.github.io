Booz Allen Hamilton — 2Q26 review

Bottom line: Miss across the board, driven by Civil segment weakness and slower funding conversion on record bookings; guidance cut confirms near-term pressure.

  • Revenue missed at $2.89B (vs. $2.97B est.), down 4.7% YoY; Adjusted EPS $1.49, down 17.7% YoY. Civil segment revenue fell 25.2% YoY on agency delays and program restructuring.
